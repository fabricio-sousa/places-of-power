What are places of power?

They are geolocations across the globe, known for their spiritual, religious and mystical significance. Some are ancient, others are modern. They are centers of energy and inspiration. They may belong to one or multiple faiths and spiritual traditions. This site is dedicated to all who seek magic in a disenchanted modern world.

How do I add a Place of Power

On the Navigation bar above, click Login, then click "Add a Place". Here you can add the name of your place, a description, and the latitude and longitude of the location. If you do not know the coordinates, you can visit Latitude to find exact latitude and longitude coordinates for the Place of Power you've visitted. Then simply paste those coordinates in the appropriate fields on the Add a Place of Power page.

Who are you?

Hi, my name is Fabricio, and I'd like to thank you for exploring. Since I was a child, I've had an interest in spirituality. In exploring the world, I noticed some places provided me with more inspiration than others. inspiration to be creative, to write, to meditate and reflect. These places were churches, parks, even my own backyard growing up in Brazil. In creating this site, I hope to catalogue places I've visited, providing some cool geocolation and wikipedia data about them. I also hope for contributions from you. The world is a vast place, and there is much to explore!
